## About

Hi! I'm Rahul, a Graduate CS student at the Manning College of Information & Computer Sciences at University of Massachusetts Amherst. 

My work and interests lie at the intersection of Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning.

## My work and research
These days, my work is majorly focused on my areas of interest. I have been working on projects related to Computer Vision and NLP areas.

During my tenure at Philips as a Software Engineer II, I successfully reduced post-release defects by 28% for the Ultrasound Eye Analytics Platform and developed a translator microservice that minimized turnaround time to less than 2 milliseconds for cached translations.
My experience at PayPal as a Software Engineer Intern further honed my skills, where I
developed an auto-remediation framework to resolve failures in Core Data Operations and created a full-stack web application using the Spring framework to monitor big data platform failures.



## My background and history
I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of
Massachusetts Amherst, maintaining a GPA of 4.0. My coursework in Computer Vision,
Advanced Natural Language Processing, Neural Networks, and Machine Learning, along with my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from BITS Pilani, has equipped me
with a strong technical background and analytical skills.
